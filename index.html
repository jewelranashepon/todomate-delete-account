How to Delete Your TodoMate Account

If you want to delete your account and all associated data from the TodoMate app, follow these steps:

  1. Open the TodoMate app
  2. Login to your account
  3. Go to the account settings screen
  4. Tap on "Delete Account" (if implemented) or
  5. Email us at support@yourdomain.com with your request

All personal data such as your name, email, and tasks will be permanently deleted from your device.
